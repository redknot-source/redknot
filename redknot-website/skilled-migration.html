Subclass 189 - Skilled Independent Visa

The Subclass 189 Skilled Independent Visa is a permanent residency visa designed for highly skilled professionals who are not sponsored by an employer, state, or family member. This visa allows you to live and work anywhere in Australia and offers a direct pathway to Australian citizenship.

Key Benefits
  • Permanent residency from the day your visa is granted.
  • Freedom to live, work, and study in any part of Australia.
  • Access to Medicare, Australia's public healthcare system.
  • Opportunity to sponsor eligible family members.
  • Pathway to apply for Australian citizenship in the future.
Who Can Apply?
  • Individuals under 45 years of age at the time of invitation.
  • Applicants with an occupation listed on Australia's Skilled Occupation List.
  • Those who receive an invitation to apply after submitting an Expression of Interest (EOI) through SkillSelect.
  • Candidates with a positive skills assessment for their nominated occupation.
  • Applicants who achieve at least 65 points on Australia's points-based system (considering factors like age, English proficiency, work experience, and qualifications).
  • Must meet health and character requirements.
Processing Time

Typically 6 to 10 months, depending on the complexity and completeness of your application.

Why Choose the Subclass 189 Visa?

This visa offers unmatched flexibility and independence — no need for employer or state sponsorship. It is ideal for skilled professionals who want the freedom to choose where they live and work while enjoying the benefits of permanent residency.

Subclass 190 - Skilled Nominated Visa

The Subclass 190 Skilled Nominated Visa is a permanent residency visa for skilled workers who are nominated by an Australian state or territory government. This visa allows you to live and work anywhere in your nominating state and offers a clear pathway to Australian citizenship.

Key Benefits
  • Permanent residency from the date your visa is granted.
  • Right to live, work, and study in Australia indefinitely.
  • Access to Medicare, Australia's public healthcare system.
  • Ability to include eligible family members in your application.
  • Opportunity to apply for Australian citizenship after fulfilling residency requirements.
Who Can Apply?
  • Individuals under 45 years of age at the time of invitation.
  • Applicants with an occupation listed on the relevant skilled occupation list for their chosen state or territory.
  • Must receive a nomination from an Australian state or territory government.
  • Submit an Expression of Interest (EOI) through SkillSelect and receive an invitation to apply.
  • Obtain a positive skills assessment for your nominated occupation.
  • Score at least 65 points on Australia's points test (factors include age, English language ability, work experience, and qualifications).
  • Meet health and character requirements.
Processing Time

Usually 6 to 9 months, depending on your individual application and nomination requirements.

Why Choose the Subclass 190 Visa?

This visa offers additional support through state nomination, which can increase your chances of receiving an invitation to apply. It is perfect for skilled professionals who are willing to live and work in a particular Australian state or territory that needs their skills.

Subclass 491 - Skilled Regional Visa

The Subclass 491 visa is a provisional visa designed for skilled workers who want to live and work in regional Australia. This visa is granted based on nomination by an Australian state or territory government or sponsorship by an eligible relative living in a designated regional area.

Key Benefits
  • Live, work, and study in regional Australia for up to 5 years.
  • Travel freely to and from Australia during the visa period.
  • Opportunity to apply for permanent residency (Subclass 191 visa) after 3 years of meeting income and residency requirements.
  • Include eligible family members in your application.
Who Can Apply?
  • Individuals under 45 years of age at the time of invitation.
  • Have an occupation listed on the relevant skilled occupation list.
  • Submit an Expression of Interest (EOI) through SkillSelect and receive a nomination or sponsorship.
  • Obtain a positive skills assessment for your nominated occupation.
  • Score at least 65 points on Australia's points test (considering age, English proficiency, work experience, and education).
  • Meet health and character requirements.
Processing Time

Usually 6 to 12 months, depending on your individual profile and nomination requirements.

Why Choose the Subclass 491 Visa?

This visa offers a valuable pathway to Australian permanent residency for those open to settling in regional areas. It increases your chances of receiving an invitation and allows you to build a future in high-demand regions across Australia.

Canada Express Entry

The Express Entry system is Canada's flagship immigration program for skilled professionals seeking permanent residency (PR). It is a fast and efficient, points-based system used by the Canadian government to select candidates for immigration based on their skills, work experience, education, and language proficiency.

Key Benefits
  • Fast-track PR processing — as quick as 6 months for eligible candidates.
  • Freedom to live, work, or study anywhere in Canada.
  • Access to public healthcare and social services.
  • Opportunity to sponsor family members.
  • Clear path to Canadian citizenship.
Who Can Apply?
  • Skilled professionals with foreign or Canadian work experience.
  • Must meet eligibility criteria for one of the three programs under Express Entry:
  • Federal Skilled Worker Program (FSWP)
  • Federal Skilled Trades Program (FSTP)
  • Canadian Experience Class (CEC)
  • A minimum Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) score based on factors such as age, education, language skills (IELTS), work experience, and adaptability.
  • Applicants must create an online Express Entry profile and receive an Invitation to Apply (ITA) for PR.
Processing Time

Typically around 6 months from receiving the ITA to final PR decision.

Express Entry Process Overview
  1. Step 1: Check eligibility and get ECA (Educational Credential Assessment).
  2. Step 2: Take a language test (IELTS/CELPIP).
  3. Step 3: Create an Express Entry profile and enter the pool.
  4. Step 4: Receive an ITA based on CRS score.
  5. Step 5: Submit a PR application and wait for approval.
Why Apply Through Redknot Consultants LLC?

Our experienced consultants simplify the entire process — from profile creation and document preparation to ITA submission and post-landing services. We ensure your application is optimized to maximize your CRS score and improve your chances of success.

New Zealand Skilled Migrant Visa

The Skilled Migrant Category Resident Visa is New Zealand's flagship program for talented professionals who want to build a future in one of the world's most beautiful and welcoming countries. This points-based visa offers skilled individuals and their families the chance to live, work, and settle permanently in New Zealand.

Key Benefits
  • Obtain permanent residency for you and your immediate family.
  • Live, work, and study anywhere in New Zealand.
  • Enjoy access to world-class healthcare and education.
  • Opportunity to eventually apply for New Zealand citizenship.
  • High quality of life and excellent work-life balance.
Who Can Apply?
  • Applicants under 55 years of age.
  • Must submit an Expression of Interest (EOI) and receive an invitation to apply.
  • Need to meet a minimum points threshold (based on age, work experience, qualifications, and job offer in New Zealand).
  • Have good English language proficiency
  • Meet health, character, and other immigration requirements.
Processing Time

Typically 8 to 12 months, depending on individual circumstances and application completeness.

Application Process Overview
  1. Check your eligibility and points score.
  2. Submit an Expression of Interest (EOI).
  3. Receive an Invitation to Apply (ITA) from Immigration New Zealand.
  4. Submit your resident visa application with all supporting documents.
  5. Await decision and prepare for your move.
Why Choose New Zealand?

New Zealand offers a unique combination of career opportunities, stunning natural landscapes, and a safe, family-friendly environment. It is one of the most desirable destinations for professionals seeking a better quality of life.
